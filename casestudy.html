Timeframe

Role

UI/UX Strategy

Platforms

Category

Social, Gaming

Streamlined Navigation

We reworked our navigation from a side drawer to a bottom tab, in order to provide a more familiar navigation to new users and to give better surface area for future projects. As we plan to add more features in the global navigation, like better friends and server discovery, we ran into way too many constraints within the existing drawer navigation. After multiple iterations, we arrived at a solution that combines the strengths of the drawer (switching between chats) and the strengths of tabs (better high-level hierarchy).

Chat

If we think about just chat, we have essentially streamlined the interface to just three core screens: the list of chats, chat, and chat detail. No more navigating through a maze of different pages and transitions. Just simple left-to-right navigation of three core screens.

Voice and Video

Discord started as the best app to get on a voice call while playing games with your friends on PC. While voice isn't used as much on mobile, it's still one of our defining features, yet a major source of user complaints. We hence took some time to establish a new, better baseline for voice and video chat on iOS and Android.
